Facilities Manager

Venn Group are supporting an organisation based in Essex with the recruitment of a Facilities Manager to effectively lead soft FM teams

  • Start Date: January
  • Duration: 12 month FTC
  • Salary: £43,500 per annum
  • Location: Essex

The successful candidate will be responsible for:

  • Hold responsibility for the day to day management of all soft FM including cleaning, security and catering services
  • Maintain and analyse weekly rosters, ensuring correct hours are worked
  • Ensure audits, inspections and surveys are reported and addressed, providing reports on SLA’s and agreed targets
  • Ensure catering services remain compliant with hygiene standard & HACCP
  • Monitor all areas for cleanliness and compliance with H & S policies/ legislation
  • Complete risk assessments for services alongside reviews on a regular basis
  • Act as central point of contact for any issues arising within soft FM services, investigating all issues in a timely manner

The successful candidate will have:

  • Experience managing facilities teams within the NHS or healthcare environment
  • A full UK driving licence and car (mileage compensated)
  • Catering management is desirable but essential
